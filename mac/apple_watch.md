# Apple Watch

## Basic controls.

### Face
Down: notifications
Up: Control center
Slow side swipe: change face

### Crown: 
edge: scroll, zoom, adjust
tap: return to watch face
hold in: Siri

### Side button
1x: recent apps
2x: Apple pay
hold on/off or 911
